About the Preclinical Imaging Core

PIC is open for researchers in NYSPI since 2016. It provides high quality equipment for multiphoton imaging and photostimulation experiments in head-fixed preparatums in vivo. PIC also has a prototyping lab (ProLab) led by Clay Lacefield for making low-cost experimental equipment with quick turnaround time.
